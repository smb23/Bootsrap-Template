Financial literacy implies a mix of mindfulness, knowledge, expertise, attitude, and behavior important to make sound money-related choices and eventually achieve individual financial prosperity. We are calling to bolster Filipino women's financial literacy by helping them upgrade their knowledge, skills, motivation, and confidence. What better way to do this than through financial education.
